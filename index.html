Yecko echoes social media posts for breaking stories by Citizen Journalists

With over seven million social media posts a minute, a lot of good content gets lost in the noise. Yecko clears this noise by providing real time social media sentiments based on global breaking stories.

What are we trying to solve?

There is alot of content on different social media platforms in the style of text,images,links to documents,videos and even live feeds. With so many social networks where people share their little stories as citizen journalists sparking the right change in society, the larger scale impact that could be further delivered missing due to the sheer number of networks and a large amount of other content on the social networks.

Imagine what a platform that enables total connection with these social networks while curating the right stories of change can do. Would something like this be good for gaining attention from governments and media to showcase, champion and take action to resolve problems? We believe so and would like to drive this change!.

The solution? Get Yecko Of course

Our mission is to build a platform that enables people to cross post information and challenges to different social media platforms, rally for a cause they care about or have faced and show case the entire journey as a time-lined story compared to a bunch of posts on social media lost in between other content.

The platform will prove as a test of time of what citizens are capable of, aiding the government and encouraging public debates to solve actual policy and public problems of utmost importance.

For version one, we are enabling our users to browse feelings from four social media websites that directly corelate to the real-time headlines feed from Google News.

Simple powerful ways to read global voices

With thousands of news headlines per hour and missions of related social media posts, we decided on having three main feature which we believe will help our users navigate with ease

Read Breaking Stories

With the help of our good friends at Google, we can fill your home feed with the latest news across nine different categories - Breaking, World, Local, Business, Technology, Entertainment, Sports, Science & Health.

The best part? you don’t have to leave the app to read these articles! Our in-app browser will link you directly to the respective site with a one click return feature.

View Social Media Reactions

With the help of Artificial Intelligence and some expert thinking by our Chief Engineer, for version one we are able to present our users with sentiments across five social media platforms - Facebook, Twitter, Instagram & Reddit.

We are searching for posts with keywords relating to the articles on your home feed. The posts are then intelligently analysed to determine if they are fake or true before being displayed for your reading pleasure..
