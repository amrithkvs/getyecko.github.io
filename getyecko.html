Yecko echoes social media posts fo breaking stories by Citizen Journalists

With over seven million social media posts a minute, a lot of good content gets lost in the noise. Yecko clears this noise by providing real time social media sentiments based on global breaking stories.

What are we trying to solve?

There is alot of content on different social media platforms in the style of text,images,links to documents,videos and even live feeds. With so many social networks where people share their little stories as citizen journalists sparking the right change in society, the larger scale impact that could be further delivered missing due to the sheer number of networks and a large amount of other content on the social networks.

Imagine what a platform that enables total connection with these social networks while curating the right stories of change can do. Would something like this be good for gaining attention from governments and media to showcase, champion and take action to resolve problems? We believe so and would like to drive this change!.

The solution? Get Yecko of course!

Our mission is to build a platform that enables people to cross post information and challenges to different social media platforms, rally for a cause they care about or have faced and show case the entire journey as a time-lined story compared to a bunch of posts on social media lost in between other content.
